Meet Kimmy

Meet Kimmy. She's a kind-hearted girl who loves her town and her planet. When she discovers litter on her favourite path, Kimmy decides it’s time to take action.

With help from her friends and her magical sparkling bag, she begins a joyful clean-up mission that inspires the whole community.

Kimmy and the Little Planet Helpers is a beautifully illustrated children’s story that teaches:

  • Respect for nature
  • The importance of not littering
  • Kindness and teamwork
  • How small actions can make a big difference

Perfect for ages 3–8, this heartwarming book helps children understand how caring for their environment helps animals, people, and the planet.

Includes a special “Facts for Parents” section to encourage learning and discussion.

Help Save Our Planet

Small actions can make a big difference

What You Can Do

🗑️ Find a bin Always put your rubbish in a bin — or take it home.
🎒 Take your litter with you No bin? Keep it in your bag until you find one.
👀 Pick up one extra piece Be a Little Planet Helper — pick up one piece you see.
🌿 Look after nature Leave parks, beaches, and bushland clean.
♻️ Recycle when you can Bottles, cans, and paper can be reused.

Why It Matters

Everything in nature is connected. When one part is harmed, it can affect many others.

Did You Know?

🌊 Over 8 million tonnes of plastic enter the ocean every year
(That’s like a truckload every minute)
🐢 More than 1 million sea animals die each year from plastic
🐦 90% of seabirds have plastic in their stomachs
🐠 Plastic breaks into tiny pieces and enters the food of sea creatures and land animals
🦀 When one species is harmed, others are affected too
🌏 Most litter starts on land and travels to the ocean

The Good News Is

Small actions from people like you can help protect our planet 🌏

Rogan and Kim Carroll are Sydney Northern Beaches locals. They love the beach and take pride in their local area.

Walking to work, or to the beach they collect the rubbish that people have discarded on the street.

“It's heartbreaking that someone's thoughtless action can have a damaging effect on our environment.” They do their bit to save the planet each day.
